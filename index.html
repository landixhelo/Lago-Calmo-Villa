ABOUT US

Enjoy Your Vacation In Our Villa And Get Lots Of Fun, Happiness And Great Pleasure

Featuring air-conditioned accommodation with a balcony, Lago Calmo Villa is located in Ksamil. With lake views, this accommodation provides a patio. The property is non-smoking and is situated 1 km from Sunset Beach.

The spacious apartment comes with 2 bedrooms, 1 bathroom, bed linen, towels, a flat-screen TV, a dining area, a fully equipped kitchen, and a terrace with mountain views. For added privacy, the accommodation has a private entrance and soundproofing.

The apartment has a picnic area where you can spend a day out in the open. Ksamil Beach is 1.2 km from the apartment, while Coco Beach is 1.5 km away. The nearest airport is Corfu International Airport, 48 km from Lago Calmo Villa.
